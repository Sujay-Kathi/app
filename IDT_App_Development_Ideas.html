üìã Project Overview

Understanding the problem and our mission

üéØ Project Title

Making Cleaning Fun and Interactive for Children - A mobile application designed to transform household chores into engaging, gamified experiences that motivate children while giving parents tools to monitor and reward their efforts.

‚ùå The Problem

  • Children find cleaning tasks dull and boring
  • Lack of motivation leads to non-participation in household chores
  • Traditional motivation methods (nagging, punishment) are ineffective
  • Parents struggle to engage children positively in cleaning activities
  • Missed opportunities to teach responsibility and life skills

üéØ Project Objectives

  • Develop a gamified mobile app that makes cleaning enjoyable
  • Foster responsibility and teamwork through collaborative tasks
  • Provide parents tools to assign, monitor, and reward cleaning efforts
  • Include educational content about cleanliness and hygiene
  • Create a safe, ad-free environment for children
